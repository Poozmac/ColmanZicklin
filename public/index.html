About our Program

Elite Group Of Students

What

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

When

July 20th, 2019 - Aug 20th 2019

Where

Zicklin business school, Baruch College

Our team is the elite of Colman College Israel. We take part in a dual Master Degree program that credits us with MBA & MS.C Entrepreneurship. Our purpose is to explore NY ecosystem, Find partnership opportunities and help to enlarge the Israel Innovation Ecosystem.

The dual degree in Entrepreneurship is designed to enable us to acquire entrepreneurial capabilities, mindset, and skills that will be helpful in advancing their future careers, and enhance their knowledge and networking abilities to create and scale their entrepreneurial companies.

The dual degree combines academic and practical knowledge, including internships in startups, online and frontal courses, and practical projects, which were all developed using the most rigorous and innovative educational models, combined with practical experience.

The courses are delivered by senior faculty members from COLLMAN and Baruch College, CUNY, who see their role as our life-mentors.
